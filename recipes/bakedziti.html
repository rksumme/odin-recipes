Bake Cauliflower Ziti

Description

The whole family will love this cheesy cauliflower baked ziti! Every bite is filled with pasta noodles coated in melted cheddar cheese, fresh herbs, and roasted cauliflower for a healthy twist.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion chopped
  • minced garlic
  • 1 lb Italian sausage
  • 2 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 28 oz crushed tomatoes
  • fresh basil to taste
  • 1 head cauliflower
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup mozzerella
  • 1/4 cup parmesan

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees
  2. Heat oil in sauce pan and saute onions
  3. Add garlic and sausage. Brown
  4. Drain, and add tomato paste, oregano, and crushed tomatoes
  5. Reduce heat and cook 10-15 minutes
  6. Cut cauliflower into pieces and boil
  7. Cool cauliflower in ice water
  8. Drain cauliflower and combine with sauce
  9. Place in two layers in baking dish with cheese on top of each layer
  10. Bake for 25 minutes
